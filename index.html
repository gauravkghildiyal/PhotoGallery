About The Website
  • The website is scalable i.e. it is operational even from small screen devices.
  • The slideshow can be opened by clicking on the SHOW button that appears when you hover over any image.
  • During the slideshow, you can use the arrow keys to glide through the images and press the Esc key to exit the slideshow.
  • The full screen button lets you switch to full screen from the website itself rather than finding the fullscreen option in your browser.
  • The simple design and 3D navigation lets you switch between pages easily.
  • For swiftness and cross browser support, the reflection of the pages that one sees during the Multi-page-display has been custom made using css and javascript without the use of any plugins or "-webkit-box-reflect".
  • The movement of the shadow of the images with the cursor movement makes the page come to life.
  • The animated background has been made using canvas animations and "puffs" (animating circles) have been mathematically modelled by making the radius and opacity an exponential function of pseudo-time for flawless transition of colors and "puffs".
  • Use of fragment identifier introduced by the hash(#) mark ensures that you can use the back button of your browser in a one page website like this which otherwise would not have been possible.
